About Us

Libera is a company specialized in trading frozen, dry and fresh fruits, vegetables and forest fruits. With huge experience in this niche our team of managers is operating on the territory of Serbia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, North Macedonia and Montenegro. With quality and stability of our services, we have proven as a reliable supplier of big companies in EU, Turkey and the Russian federation. Our challenges are new markets that we are extending gradually. Because of the constant expansion, our company motto is: "High-quality products, professionalism, and satisfied customers".
